# 社区行为准则

[English](#community-code-of-conduct-in-english) · 中文

码喵社区欢迎不同经验、背景与表达方式的贡献者。参与讨论、Issue、评审和代码贡献时，请：

- 围绕事实与代码讨论，尊重不同意见；
- 给出可执行、具体且友善的反馈；
- 不骚扰、不歧视、不公开他人的私人信息；
- 对安全与隐私问题保持克制，先走私密报告渠道；
- 发现误会时先澄清，再下结论。

维护者可以编辑、隐藏或移除违反这些原则的内容，并在严重或重复违规时限制参与。需要报告社区行为问题时，请通过仓库维护者提供的私密渠道联系。

---

## Community Code of Conduct in English

The Miao community welcomes contributors with different experience, backgrounds, and communication styles. In issues, reviews, discussions, and code contributions:

- discuss facts and code while respecting disagreement;
- give specific, actionable, and kind feedback;
- do not harass, discriminate, or expose another person's private information;
- report security and privacy concerns through private channels first;
- clarify misunderstandings before drawing conclusions.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates these principles and may restrict participation after serious or repeated violations. Report conduct concerns through a private maintainer channel.
